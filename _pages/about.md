## About me

I am a Master’s student in Financial Technology at Washington University in St. Louis, with a background in Computer Engineering from The Pennsylvania State University.

My academic and professional interests focus on data analytics, systems development, and machine learning. I am particularly interested in building data-driven systems that combine solid engineering foundations with practical analytical and modeling techniques.

During my undergraduate and graduate studies, I have worked across data engineering, software development, and machine learning. My experience includes analyzing large-scale log and sensor data, building ETL pipelines using Python and SQL, and developing backend services and dashboards for data visualization. I also have hands-on experience with deep learning models, including image super-resolution approaches implemented with PyTorch.

Previously, I worked as a Game Testing Intern at Coconut Island Games, where I analyzed gameplay logs to identify performance bottlenecks and crash patterns, and supported regression and performance testing workflows. I also completed a Software Development Internship at Jiangsu Delauney Information Co., Ltd., where I contributed to automating CAD drawing workflows using C++ and Python-based services integrated with LLM APIs.

I am currently seeking opportunities related to data analytics, machine learning, and software or systems development, where I can apply my technical background to solve real-world problems and continue to grow as an engineer.

## 关于我

我目前是华盛顿大学圣路易斯分校（Washington University in St. Louis）金融科技（Financial Technology）硕士研究生，本科毕业于宾夕法尼亚州立大学（The Pennsylvania State University），专业为计算机工程（Computer Engineering）。

我的学习与研究方向主要集中在数据分析、系统开发以及机器学习。我对将扎实的工程基础与实际的数据分析和建模方法相结合、构建数据驱动型系统尤为感兴趣。

在本科和研究生阶段的学习过程中，我在数据工程、软件开发和机器学习等多个方向积累了实践经验，包括对大规模日志和传感器数据的分析、使用 Python 和 SQL 构建 ETL 数据处理流程，以及开发用于数据可视化的后端服务和仪表盘。同时，我也具备一定的深度学习实践经验，曾使用 PyTorch 实现图像超分辨率相关模型。

在实习经历方面，我曾在 Coconut Island Games 担任游戏测试实习生，主要负责分析游戏运行日志以定位性能瓶颈和崩溃模式，并参与回归测试和性能测试相关工作。此外，我还曾在江苏狄诺尼信息技术有限公司担任软件开发实习生，参与使用 C++ 与 Python 服务自动化 CAD 绘图流程，并结合大语言模型（LLM）API 完成相关系统集成。

目前，我正在寻找与数据分析、机器学习以及软件或系统开发相关的机会，希望能够将自身的工程背景与数据与智能技术相结合，解决实际问题，并持续提升自身的技术深度与工程能力。
